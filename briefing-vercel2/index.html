Four Domains of Integrative Briefing

Rather than a linear sequence, these domains form a continuous cycle. Insights from inhabitation feed back into strategy.

→ → → ↺
◈

Strategic Domain

Purpose, values and long-term direction. Divergent thinking: exploring possibilities, asking "why?", framing the challenge. Draws on leadership, context, constraints and a clear view of what you value most.

◇

Conceptual Domain

Translating strategy into testable design ideas. Convergent thinking on spatial, organisational and technological possibilities. Precedent research, testing ideas before committing, resolving fundamental tensions.

▣

Production Domain

Detailed design and delivery. Risk management, layering decisions by timeframe (fixed/intermediate/fluid), specifications. Ensuring early strategic intent is not lost as the project moves into delivery.

○

Inhabitation & Evaluation Domain

Post-occupancy learning and closing the loop. What did we learn? How do inhabitants shape the space over time? How does evaluation feed back into the next strategic cycle?
